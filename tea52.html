Tea 52

Tea 52 is an organic tea brand targeting a youthful audience that enjoys acquiring "aesthetic" but high-quality products. For the design of this packaging, I combined two things that I believe have nothing in common but seemed interesting to explore: tea and playing cards. The packaging, when viewed from the front, has a composition similar to that of a playing card. I developed this design in its print-ready format with the box pattern, taking into account the margin, bleed, and glue areas.
