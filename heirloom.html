Heirloom Seeds

I developed the branding for the Heirloom Seeds brand. For the logo, I based it on the concept of a relic, giving the seed a touch of "royalty" and elegance with the surrounding frame. For the typography, I chose a font that balances that elegance with a more relaxed yet readable style. I also worked on a catalog featuring all the seeds they have for sale, ready for printing and distribution to potential customers.
