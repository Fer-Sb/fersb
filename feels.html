Feels Zine

For an InDesign class project, we were tasked with selecting a magazine that intrigued us and crafting an article that adhered to its editorial design, paying close attention to typesetting details. I chose @feelszine because I admire their focus on exploring emotions, and I particularly enjoyed a 2019 piece by @wandering_gaia titled "Why Good People Turn Bad Online." In InDesign, I meticulously handled the text, pull quotes, alignment, and hyphenation. I used Procreate to create expressive doodles on the images, which I first edited in Photoshop.
